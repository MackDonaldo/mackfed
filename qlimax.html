Qlimax the reawakening

A new ritual awaits on November 20th. Prepare to embrace your destiny at Qlimax The Reawakening. Come with us as we embark on a journey that transcends this reality. Dance with us into the unknown. Will you join the High Priests and become a disciple?

For the first time ever, Qlimax will take place during the day from 12:00 until 23:00 with a maximum capacity of 75% due to restrictions by the Dutch government. It will be a unique experience that lets you undergo the rites of Qlimax in a whole new way.

Ticket sales for Qlimax The Reawakening start on October 2nd (13:00 CEST), the travel sale starts on September 25rd (13:00 CEST).*
